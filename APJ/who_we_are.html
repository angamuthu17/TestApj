A Purposeful Journey is a brainchild of group of friends, who came up with thought of saving a small amount every month and give it back to society on need basis during summer 2015.

It has evolved and inspired many of their friends to join and continue contributing for positive social causes.

A Purposeful Journey was registered in 2017 as public charitable trust at Madurai, Tamil Nadu, India.

As a trust, it continuous its journey to help the people on need with their supporting activities.
